# Adjoint Subring

Elements $$ \mathbf{1}, \mathbf{2}, \mathbf{3}, \mathbf{4}, \mathbf{5}, \mathbf{6} $$, form the [ adjoint subring ]({% link pages/Concepts/AdjointBasedSubring.md %})[ $$ \text{FR}^{6,0}_{8} $$ ]({% link pages/FRPages/FR_6_1_0_8.md %}).

The [ upper central series ]({% link pages/Concepts/AdjointBasedSubring.md %}) is the following:
$$
\text{FR}^{8,0}_{14} \underset{ \mathbf{1}, \mathbf{2}, \mathbf{3}, \mathbf{4}, \mathbf{5}, \mathbf{6} }{\supset}  \text{FR}^{6,0}_{8}
$$

# Universal grading

Each particle can be graded as follows: $$ \text{deg}(\mathbf{1}) = \mathbf{1}', \text{deg}(\mathbf{2}) = \mathbf{1}', \text{deg}(\mathbf{3}) = \mathbf{1}', \text{deg}(\mathbf{4}) = \mathbf{1}', \text{deg}(\mathbf{5}) = \mathbf{1}', \text{deg}(\mathbf{6}) = \mathbf{1}', \text{deg}(\mathbf{7}) = \mathbf{2}', \text{deg}(\mathbf{8}) = \mathbf{2}' $$, where the degrees form the group $$ \mathbb{Z}_2 $$ with multiplication table:

$$
\begin{array}{|ll|}
\hline
 \mathbf{1}' & \mathbf{2}' \\
 \mathbf{2}' & \mathbf{1}' \\
\hline
\end{array}
$$
