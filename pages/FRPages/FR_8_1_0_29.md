# Categorifications
The gauging process gives a categorification of the fusion ring $\text{FR}^{8,0}_{29}$ and possibly its siblings. One takes the 3-fermion theory $\text{SO}(8)_1$ and gauges a global $S_3$ symmetry, producing a rank-12, dimension-144 modular category. Taking the trivial component of its universal $\mathbf{Z}_2$ grading gives a rank 8 dimension 72 ribbon fusion category, which has fusion ring $\text{FR}^{8,0}_{29}$. See refs [^1][^2] for more details.
