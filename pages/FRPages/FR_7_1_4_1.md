# Representations of $SL_2(\mathbb{Z})$

This fusion ring does not provide any representations of $SL_2(\mathbb{Z}).$

# Adjoint Subring

Elements $$ \mathbf{1}, \mathbf{2}, \mathbf{3}, \mathbf{4}, \mathbf{5}, \mathbf{6} $$, form the [ adjoint subring ]({% link pages/Concepts/AdjointBasedSubring.md %})[ $$ \mathbb{Z}_6:\ \text{FR}^{6,4}_{1} $$ ]({% link pages/FRPages/FR_6_1_4_1.md %}).

The [ upper central series ]({% link pages/Concepts/AdjointBasedSubring.md %}) is the following:
$$
\left.\text{TY(}\mathbb{Z}_2\times \mathbb{Z}_3\right) \underset{ \mathbf{1}, \mathbf{2}, \mathbf{3}, \mathbf{4}, \mathbf{5}, \mathbf{6} }{\supset}  \mathbb{Z}_6 \underset{ \mathbf{1} }{\supset}  \text{Trivial}
$$
