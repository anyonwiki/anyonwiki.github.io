# Fusion Rules

$$
\begin{array}{|llll|}
\hline
 \mathbf{1} & \mathbf{2} & \mathbf{3} & \mathbf{4} \\
 \mathbf{2} & \mathbf{1} & \mathbf{3} & \mathbf{4} \\
 \mathbf{3} & \mathbf{3} & \mathbf{1}+\mathbf{2}+\mathbf{4} & \mathbf{3}+\mathbf{4} \\
 \mathbf{4} & \mathbf{4} & \mathbf{3}+\mathbf{4} & \mathbf{1}+\mathbf{2}+\mathbf{3} \\
\hline
\end{array}
$$

$$ \{(\mathbf{3} \  \mathbf{4})\} $$
